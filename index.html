OtHello Kitty Rules

Othello, also known as Reversi is an 8x8 strategy board game with two players.
Now, you can play with ChocoCat and Hello Kitty, too!

  • Choose which color you would like to be.
  • Black goes first.
  • Find a piece of your opponent's color on the board.
  • Look at the row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal) and make sure one of your pieces is also in that row.
  • Place your piece. Your opponent's pieces will flip, and you gain points!
  • The number of points you have are the number of coins you have on the board.
  • The player with the most pieces on the board by the end of the game wins!
